# Design System: Corporate Partnership UI

## 1. Typography

The UI uses a combination of a high-contrast Serif for authority and a clean, geometric Sans-Serif for readability and modern appeal.

| Element | Font Category | Suggested Font | Details |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Headings (H1, H2)** | Serif | **Playfair Display** or **Lora** | Semi-bold, high contrast, elegant. |
| **Body / Labels** | Sans-Serif | **Inter** or **Montserrat** | Clean, geometric, used for card subtitles and buttons. |
| **Quotes** | Serif | **Playfair Display** | Italicized for the testimonial section. |

---

## 2. Color Palette

The palette relies on a "Deep Forest" green paired with "Antique White" to avoid the harshness of pure black and white.

### Primary Colors

* **Deep Forest (Backgrounds):** `#1A1C19` (A very dark, desaturated green/black)
* **Vibrant Green (Accents/CTAs):** `#4B9400` (Used for primary buttons and card headers)
* **Gold/Brass (Secondary CTA):** `#C5A367` (Used for "Become a Partner" button)

### Neutral Colors

* **Off-White (Background):** `#F9F7F2` (Warm, paper-like tone)
* **Card Background:** `#FFFFFF` (Pure white for contrast against the off-white)
* **Text (Dark):** `#2D2D2D`
* **Text (Muted):** `#666666`

---

## 3. UI Components

### Hero / Footer Sections

* **Background:** Deep Forest (`#1A1C19`).
* **Text Color:** White or very light grey.
* **Alignment:** Center-aligned.

### Partnership Cards

* **Container:** White background, subtle 1px border (`#E0E0E0`), and a soft drop shadow.
* **Header Accent:** A thick 4px top border in Vibrant Green (`#4B9400`).
* **Logo:** Centered, fixed height (approx. 40-50px).
* **Buttons:** * **Call Button:** Solid Vibrant Green with white text.
* **Reference Button:** Ghost button (outline) with a thin beige/gold border.



### Testimonial Section

* **Quote Icon:** Large, serif quotation marks in Gold (`#C5A367`).
* **Style:** Italicized Serif text, centered, with high line-height () for readability.

---

## 4. Spacing & Layout

* **Content Width:** Max-width of 1200px for desktop.
* **Section Padding:** Large vertical padding (80px - 100px) to create "breathable" whitespace.
* **Grid:** 3-column responsive grid for the partnership cards.

---

## 5. Assets & Styling

* **Border Radius:** 8px for cards and buttons (softened corners).
* **Shadows:** `box-shadow: 0 4px 20px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.05);` (Very subtle).
* **Iconography:** Use clean, corporate logos with original brand colors (Eskom Blue, VW Dark Blue, Momentum Red).
